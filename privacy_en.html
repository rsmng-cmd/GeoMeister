🌍 GeoMeister — Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025

Developer: Ares  |  Contact: aresmungan@gmail.com

This Privacy Policy explains how GeoMeister ("the App") collects, uses, and protects your personal data.

1. Data We Collect

  • Account information: Username and email address (during registration)
  • Game data: Scores, levels, statistics and ranking information
  • Technical data: Device type, OS version (for performance optimization)
  • Ad data: Google AdMob may collect anonymous data for ad personalization

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To personalize and improve your game experience
  • To manage leaderboards and ranking systems
  • To provide multiplayer game features
  • To detect and resolve technical issues
  • To display advertisements (via Google AdMob)

3. Third-Party Services

  • Google Firebase: Authentication and database — Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Google AdMob: Ad display — Google Privacy Policy

These services may collect data under their own privacy policies.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is securely stored on Google Firebase infrastructure. When you delete your account, your data is removed from our systems. We do not sell or rent your data to third parties.

5. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If we become aware of such data, we will delete it immediately.

6. Your Rights (GDPR)

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Rectify inaccurate personal data
  • Request erasure of your personal data
  • Object to processing of your personal data
  • Data portability

To exercise these rights, email us at aresmungan@gmail.com.

7. Ad Preferences

To opt out of personalized ads, use your device's ad settings. On Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization.

8. Policy Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Significant changes will be notified within the app. The current policy is always available on this page.

9. Contact

For questions about our privacy policy:
